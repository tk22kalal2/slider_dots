    Beware of Online Education Scammers

    With the rise of online education, many students are seeking quality learning platforms. Unfortunately, not all platforms are trustworthy. Scammers are exploiting this growing trend, offering fake courses, certifications, and even degrees. Hereâ€™s how to identify and protect yourself from online education scams.

    TYPE 1 :- NO CONTENT

    • People who do not have any education content like education videos, notes, or books but pretend to have them, and ask users for money in exchange for the content. As the user pays them money, they block the user without giving any content and scam them. Be aware of such scammers. Don't take such risks.

    TYPE 2 :- BRAIN WASH PEOPLE AND SCAM

    • Some people provide free content in a channel which expires in a day. No one is able to study from the content and gets frustrated. Then the admin of the channel starts providing a paid channel. With such frustration, users buy paid content from the admin.

    TYPE 3 :- SELLING FREE LINKS

    • Some people sell channel links which provide free videos and notes. The channels are already free, but these scammers sell the links as paid.

    RG KAR Case

    The RG Kar case refers to the brutal rape and murder of a trainee doctor at RG Kar Medical College and Hospital in Kolkata. The victim's body was found with multiple injury marks, including bleeding from her eyes, mouth, and private parts, indicating a violent and brutal murder after sexual assault.

    VICTIM DETAILS

    • Victim's Age: The victim was a 25-year-old trainee doctor.
    • Occupation: She was a postgraduate medical student.
    • Institution: She was training at RG Kar Medical College and Hospital in Kolkata.
    • Date of Incident: The incident occurred in August 2024.

    CASE TIMELINE

    • August 2024: The incident occurred, sparking widespread protests and outrage.
    • September 9, 2024: The Supreme Court ordered protesting doctors to return to work and directed the CBI to file a fresh status report.
    • September 14, 2024: The CBI arrested the former principal of RG Kar Medical College and a police officer for allegedly tampering with evidence and delaying the FIR.

    INVESTIGATION AND ARRESTS

    • The CBI took over the investigation, and arrests were made, including the former principal, Sandip Ghosh, and a police officer, Abhijit Mondal, for destruction of evidence and delay in filing the FIR.
